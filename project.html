Ongoing Projects

Combinatorial Multi-armed Bandit Mechanisms

MATRICS has awarded a research project titled 'Characterizing and Designing Combinatorial Multi-armed Bandit Mechanisms.' The project explores innovative approaches to optimize decision-making processes with applications in diverse fields.

PI: Dr. Shweta Jain

Learning in Dynamic and Strategic Environments

The Core Research Grant (CRG) has funded the research project 'Strategic Learning: Understanding Decision-Making with Strategic Agents.' This study investigates decision-making processes in scenarios with strategic actors who manipulate their feature space to get most favorable machine learning outcomes, providing valuable insights applicable across various domains.

PI: Dr. Ganesh Ghalme

Co-PI: Dr. Shweta Jain

Fair Federated Learning

The Core Research Grant (CRG) has granted funding to the project "Advancing Fairness in Federated Learning Models." This research aims to improve fairness within federated learning, making a significant contribution to equitable and ethical AI.

PI: Dr. Sujit Gujar

Co-PI: Dr. Shweta Jain

Human-in-the-loop

Human-in-the-loop projects leverage the synergy between human intelligence and automated systems to enhance decision-making, problem-solving, and overall efficiency.

PI: Dr. Shweta Jain

Co-PI: Dr. Shashi Shekhar Jha

Completed Projects

Fairness and Interpretability in Machine Learning

The SERB-SRG grant funds research aiming to construct machine learning models that prioritize fairness and interpretability, thereby addressing ethical and transparency concerns in the field of AI. This grant underscores the importance of advancing responsible and comprehensible AI technologies, with project outcomes recognized and published in various high-impact factor journals and prestigious CORE A* / A conferences.

PI: Dr. Shweta Jain
