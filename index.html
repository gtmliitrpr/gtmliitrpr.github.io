About Us

GTML Lab, an initiative by the visionary Dr Shweta Jain, stands as a pioneering initiative at the crossroads of Game Theory and Machine Learning. Our unwavering dedication revolves around harnessing the synergies between game theory and artificial intelligence, with the ultimate aim of tackling real-world issues. Our mission is to unlock the realms of machine learning, algorithmic game theory, and their pragmatic applications. Through strategic collaborations with esteemed global organizations and collaborators, we actively strive to effect substantial change in vital sectors such as healthcare, finance, and beyond. We invite you to embark on this journey with us, as we collectively shape the future of decision-making and problem-solving. Join us in forging a path toward a brighter and more innovative world.
